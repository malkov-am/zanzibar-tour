Discovering underwater world

With living reefs surrounding the islands, Zanzibar is a prime dive destination. The water temperature is warm, visibility is usually excellent, currents are weak and there’s an abundance of colourful fish

Jozani Forest and rare animals

Zanzibar is home to the red colobus monkey, the Zanzibar servaline genet, and the Zanzibar leopard. Jozani Forest- is a place, where we will see them

Nungwi village

Nungwi village is a small, traditional village. You will have the possibility to explore this traditional fishing village with visiting the local boatyard, where fisherman’s dhows are still made the traditional way.
